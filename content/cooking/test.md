# chicken corn chowder

## ingredients
- 2 strips bacon
- 4 chicken thighs
- garlic powder
- dried italian herb mix
- salt
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 1 large potato
- 3/4 cup half and half
- 4 oz cottage cheese (optional)
- 1/4 cup cheddar cheese
- 1 tbsp tomato paste

## steps
1. season chicken with garlic powder, herbs, and salt
2. cook the bacon in a soup pot
3. remove the bacon and add olive oil
4. sear the chicken
5. stir in tomato paste
6. add broth and potato
7. let simmer for 30min-1h
8. add frozen corn
9. blend half and half with cottage cheese
10. stir in half and half mixture and cheddar cheese
11. serve!
